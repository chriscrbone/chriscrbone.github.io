Fortinet SOC Test Environment

Our capstone project focuses on designing and implementing a test environment to evaluate a Fortinet Security Operations Center (SOC) for the University of New Haven. The project will provide a controlled environment to simulate real-world network traffic, security events, and threats, thus enabling the configuration and testing of Fortinet security tools. By integrating monitoring, detection, and response capabilities, the SOC will serve as both a research and educational resource for students and faculty. This initiative aims to strengthen the university’s cybersecurity infrastructure while giving students hands-on experience in managing enterprise-grade security solutions.
